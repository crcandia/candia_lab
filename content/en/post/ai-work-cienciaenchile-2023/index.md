Ciencia en Chile published Cristian Esteban Candia Vallejos' opinion column on automation, artificial intelligence, labor markets, and the need to treat technological change as both a risk and an opportunity.

The piece emphasizes responsible technology adoption, lifelong learning, transferable skills, and institutional preparation for uncertain labor-market transformations.
