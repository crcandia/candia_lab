Universidad del Desarrollo highlighted **Lixandria**, an AI-based platform created by Cristian Candia-Castro to help students and school counselors explore, compare, and expand academic alternatives in higher education.

The project connects revealed preferences, network science, educational data, and recommender systems to make the Chilean higher education landscape easier to navigate.
