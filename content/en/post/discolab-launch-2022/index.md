Universidad del Desarrollo announced the launch of **Discolab**, a collaborative reading, annotation, and deliberation platform created by Cristian Candia-Castro.

The launch positioned Discolab as a tool for improving the understanding of complex texts through comments, votes, rankings, consensus signals, and controversy detection.
