<p style="text-align: center;">CRiSS-LAB is the Computational Research in Social Science Lab at Universidad del Desarrollo in Santiago, Chile, directed by Cristian Candia, Ph.D. We study social systems where information circulates, changes form, and shapes decisions: science, education, culture, mobility, organizations, markets, and public debate.</p>

<p style="text-align: center;">Our strength is integrating social theory, dynamic models, network science, causal inference, applied AI, and large-scale behavioral data. This combination helps explain how collective patterns emerge and translates that knowledge into platforms such as Lixandria, Discolab, PriorizaChile, SocialRec, MúsicaCL, DYNAMAP, and Capybara.</p>

<p style="text-align: right;"><em>Cristian Candia, Ph.D.</em><br><em>Head of CRiSS-LAB.</em></p>
