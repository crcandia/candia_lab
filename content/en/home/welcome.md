<p style="text-align: center;">CRiSS-LAB is the Computational Research in Social Science Lab at Universidad del Desarrollo in Santiago, Chile, directed by Cristian Candia, Ph.D. We investigate collective intelligence, collective memory, education, school coexistence, mobility, cooperation, polarization, culture, organizations, markets, science, and institutional decision-making as interconnected social systems.</p>

<p style="text-align: center;">Our work combines applied AI, network science, causal inference, machine learning, recommender systems, experimental game theory, computational text analysis, and large-scale behavioral data. We connect this research with applied platforms such as Lixandria, Discolab, PriorizaChile, SocialRec, MúsicaCL, DYNAMAP, and Capybara, translating computational social science into usable evidence for students, schools, public institutions, organizations, business applications, and civic debate.</p>

<p style="text-align: right;"><em>Cristian Candia, Ph.D.</em><br><em>Head of CRiSS-LAB.</em></p>
