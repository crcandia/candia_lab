<p style="text-align: center;">CRiSS-LAB is the Computational Research in Social Science Lab at Universidad del Desarrollo in Santiago, Chile, directed by Cristian Candia, Ph.D. We study how abundant information becomes collectively relevant: what receives attention, what is remembered, how preferences take shape, and how decisions become coordinated action.</p>

<p style="text-align: center;">Our work connects social theory, network science, dynamic models, causal inference, AI, and experimental platforms to generate evidence for education, science, culture, organizations, public policy, and digital environments.</p>
