<p style="text-align: center;">CRiSS-LAB is the Computational Research in Social Science Lab at Universidad del Desarrollo in Santiago, Chile, directed by Cristian Candia, Ph.D. We study how people, groups, institutions, and societies decide what matters in environments saturated with data, AI, and information.</p>

<p style="text-align: center;">Our work examines how attention, memory, preferences, and coordination shape collective relevance: what becomes visible, what is remembered, what is preferred, and how decisions become coordinated action. We combine computational social science, network science, dynamic models, causal inference, AI, and experimental platforms to generate evidence for education, science, culture, organizations, public policy, and digital environments.</p>
